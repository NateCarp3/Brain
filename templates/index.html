Why 3D Print The Brain?

  1. Currently, residential training and surgical planning rely almost exclusively on 2-dimensional Magnetic Resonance Images (MRI).

  2. Surgical preparedness could be advanced with 3-dimensional models.

  3. 3D Printed Brain Models can be used by doctors to communicate with patients about upcoming procedures

  4. 3D printed models could inspire future generations of brain surgeons

  5. Can be used as a symbol for mental health
